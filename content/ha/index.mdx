title: High Availability
---

<a id="ha-label"></a>

<a id="op-cluster-resiliency"></a>


Kinetica High Availability (HA) eliminates the single point of failure in a
standard Kinetica cluster and provides recovery from failure.  It does this via
*ring resiliency*, by grouping clusters together and utilizing *RabbitMQ*,
*Etcd*, and data replication to achieve a consistent data store across clusters.
